Our History



Since 1992, Mirna's Cafe offers a lavish and expansive menu comprised of the finest in Italian & Mediterranean dishes, from creamy homemade hummus and the area’s authentic fish and pasta dishes, to chicken and veal which all cooked to order. Our tasty specials from our famous frenched rack of lamb, crab cakes, our seafood jambalaya and many many more are expertly prepared and final analysis, are the primary reasons why Mirna’s Café reigns supreme as the ultimate in Montgomery County PA’s’ Eclectic Italian Mediterranean cuisine.



Mirna’s Chefs with the dedicated help of our kitchen employees specialize in preparing the most famous recipes that were collected from the Italian & Mediterranean region of the world and prepared especially for you in our humble kitchen.
