Your Own Private Section

Celebrate the occasion in your own private section! We provide our medium size room and or our larger dining area to fit the needs of your celebration. We are happy to rearrange the dining room to fit the design of your celebration.

Choose Your Own Menu

You are free to pick your favorite dishes from our large menu into your own party menu! We are pleased to modify any dish to the needs of your guests. Enjoy a four course meal to celebrate the occasion.

Decorate To Your Likings

Decorate the private area to the theme of your party! We offer numerous colored napkins to fit the aesthetic of your design. Your wish is our command to make the occasion as memorable as possible.

Enjoy Drinks At Our Bar

Get served splendid drinks at our bar area! We are happy to serve your guests any drink you bring in. We also provide unlimited fountain drinks in our private party package.

Our Signature Hors D'oeuvres

Enjoy our exquisite hors d'oeuvres as guests filter into the dining room! Our bruschetta and spinach feta filo are delicious starters that are passed around until guests have a chance to get their seats.
