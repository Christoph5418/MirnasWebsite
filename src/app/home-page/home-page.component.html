Welcome to Mirna's Cafe



An esteemed dining establishment with rich heritage, serving Mediterranean and Italian cuisine since 1992

Since 1992, Mirna’s Restaurant & Grill has provided the Montgomery County area with Italian & Mediterranean food with quality second to none. It remains one of the best eclectic Italian & Mediterranean cuisine in the area. From their appetizers to entrees, sandwiches to desserts, the entire menu is home-made with only the freshest of ingredients. We offer a lavish and expansive menu comprised of the finest in Italian & Mediterranean dishes, from creamy homemade hummus and the area’s authentic fish and pasta dishes, to chicken and veal which are all cooked to order.
